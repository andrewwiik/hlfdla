Highland Lakes is a small community and our Fire Department is entirely volunteer. While Vernon Township does provide some funding it does not assist with many of the expenses to run and maintain the physical building or numerous other expenses necessary to operate the department so that the firefighters are able to effectively respond when the unfortunate need arises. These expenses are paid for through donations and fundraisers, like our annual Tricky Tray, held throughout the year.

The Highland Lakes Fire Department Ladies Auxiliary is a 501(c)3 organization. The non-profit status of the Highland Lakes Fire Department Ladies Auxiliary can be verified at http://apps.irs.gov/app/pub78 .
